The archetype of primordial collective residence that, from antiquity to today, was the symbol of union and sharing throughout the planet: the PATIO.

The CENTRAL SPACE of the patio will become the fundamental element of the project as a central place for SOCIALIZATION AND SHARING among the users of the center.

For the resolution of this last point, the use of PARAMETRIC GENERATIVE DESIGN has been considered useful in the preliminary phase for the definition of the shape of the building and then to obtain the best result for the armor system.

In the typology of Hispanic and Latin American patio houses, vegetation is a fundamental pawn both as a decorative element and as a shield against excessive solar irradiation. The vines, particularly the deciduous ones with flowers, have been taken as a concept for the development of the shading element of the building.

Key process

The typology of the courtyard presents a single defect, that is, it identifies the central space as the only common environment only at the entrance level.

In order to value the concepts that represent this space, it is necessary to think about an evolution of the patio, redistributing it in MORE POSSIBLE LEVELS.

The resulting project therefore takes up both the composition and social relationship aspects of the residential typology in Patio Espanico, re-proposing them in a modern and sustainable key.
